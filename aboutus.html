ABOUT SPFP

What is Shallow Pocket Farm Park

This is a budget friendly park farm where you can come and relax, eat, walk and see some animals. We are a functioning farm that deal with live stock, dairy and vegitables but we also enjoy educating and entertaining people and there families and give you more reasons to get out the house. People passing through dumfries can also pop in for a bite to eat on there travels. All are welcome.

What can we do at SPFP?

The range of things to do here are from just simply walking the routs and seeing the sites and animals roaming along with you. We also have a small zoo where you can pet some animals feed others or just watch as they go about there business. Our cafe is a great place to get some food. We do breakfasts and home cooked meals like roast beef dinners and chicken soups and after you have walked, seen the animals and had dinner, you can visit our shop to take away some lovely plants and some produce to stock your cupboards and bring some nature into your home.

Cafe Bookings & info

There are limited seating within the Cafe so for that reason if you would like to sit in it is recomended that you book a seat for your party in advance. There is plenty of space and seating outside if you dont book a table. We would apreciat it if you have booked a table and cant make it, if you would kindly cancle with us so we can open that booking for another party. It not necessary to book a table as we will offer you a table if one is free on your arrival.
