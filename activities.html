Here at SPFP

We haver a great range of activities. has a great range of animals to look at. Some of them are from this region but some are rescue animals from people who no longer could look after them or did not want them anymore, like PEPO the Penguin!
Our walking routs are good for many differant types of walkers. The scenic routs are good for shorter walks with your dogs and the hill climbs are for those looking to excercise with purpose then theres here you can enjoy some seriously good food. Its very homely cooking, our roast dinners are the same as the ones we would cook for our family so you can be assured quality grub after your nice day out. The play park we have just nearby the cafe will allow you to sit and eat in whilst the kids go off and play, giving you parents some well deserved rest. There something for everyone here at our Shallow Pocket Farm Park


Our Zoo

The zoo we have is a little differant to what you would expect. We have some odd animals here. You can feed some of the animals and pet others. We take donations for helping keep the animals fed and watered. Visit our zoo page to learn more about them and see what we have.

Walking Routs

The walking routs we have are a great way to keep fit and enjoy the outdoors for all the family. We have simple scenic routs for you to enjoy, walking the dogs and seeing what animals we have and some hill walks that are great for fitness and cardio excersise. Then afterwards you can go to our cafe and enjoy some food.

The shop

The shop we have sells produce and plants, all of which is organic and farmed on site. We also use these in our cafe to make delicious and hearty meals for everyone. Take home some food home for the family dinner. The plants we have on sale are beautiful and vibrant plants that you can use in the home to lighten up the enviroment and also some for for outside if you want to spritz up the garden for summer.

Cafe Bookings & Info

There are limited seating within the Cafe so for that reason if you would like to sit in it is recomended that you book a seat for your party in advance. There is plenty of space and seating outside if you dont book a table. We would apreciat it if you have booked a table and cant make it, if you would kindly cancle with us so we can open that booking for another party. It not necessary to book a table as we will offer you a table if one is free on your arrival.
