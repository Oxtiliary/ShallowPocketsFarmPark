Shallow Pockets FarmPark

Welcome to SPFP. Here we are a family run farm with a passion for animals and entertainment. Here you can enjoy a day out with your family doing a variety of activities including such things like our small zoo, walking the routs and eating in at our cafe... or out. Click the button bellow to learn more about what we have to offer.

Our Shop

Here at our shop we sell our own home grown and homemade plants and produce. The plants we sell are grown in our large green houses so poeple can take these lovely plants back as gifts for family and friends. We deliver top quality fruits and veg for people to take away at a reasonable price. We also use these in our cafe for well made homely dinners.

Our Cafe

In our cafe we make home made dinners and breakfasts. We use our own home grown produce and meats to cook up some delicious foods for families and for people just passing by. We have seating for large groups both inside and out for family dinners and small seating areas for those looking for a quiet place to eat.

Our Zoo

Our zoo is a highlight of anyones visit. We have a great range of animals that have been saved from people who didnt want them anymore or could not take care of them anymore. Here they are well taken care of and some of them are nice enough to pet. We also have our own born and bred animals native to our homeland that love to be fed and petted by the people who visit. Have a look at our zoo page to learn more about our animals and donations.

Cafe Bookings & Info

There are limited seating within the Cafe so for that reason if you would like to sit in it is recomended that you book a seat for your party in advance. There is plenty of space and seating outside if you dont book a table. We would apreciat it if you have booked a table and cant make it, if you would kindly cancle with us so we can open that booking for another party. It not necessary to book a table as we will offer you a table if one is free on your arrival.
