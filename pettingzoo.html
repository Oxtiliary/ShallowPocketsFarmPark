The Zoo

The petting zoo we have is special, its where people can come and see some animals and relax. Feeding the animals and petting them are a great we to get the kids out and about and also learn somethings. We have some weird and wonderful creatures here because poeple get some exotic animals and find out later on they can no longer look after them, so we come along and take them off there hands and give them a home. We take donations to help look after the animals here and also you can buy food from our to feed them. This will help keep them fed and us as well. The zoo is a good place to go before having dinner at our or after going a walk around out site. You can see some animals in there natural enviromant when going along some of the walks we have set out.
We also have a teaching room in our zoo that will show and tell poeple about some animals they might not know about or know little about. Its worth looking at, there are some interesting thigs there to be seen!

Farm Animals

We have the usual but not un interesting farm animals. The cows, sheep, goats and pigs. They love poeple and love to eat their heart out. The pigs are especially friendly to those with apples!

Lesser Known Creatures

Here we will mention some of the animals we have had to take from poeple who cant look after them. We have some Penguins, monkeys and some peacocks. The peacocks are roaming free around the farm so you might just bump into one on your travels.
